# Miauudio Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026.

Miauudio is a free ambient sound mixer developed by MichalMatu. This policy
covers the Android app and the web/PWA version.

## Data handled by the Android app

Miauudio does not require an account and does not include advertising or
analytics. The developer does not receive your mixes, presets, favorites,
settings, generator configurations, notes, todos, or imported audio.
The Android app does not request internet access.

Preferences are stored locally in the app's browser storage. Audio that you
explicitly import is validated and copied into app-private storage. Imported
files are not uploaded by Miauudio.

Miauudio does not request health permissions, connect to Health Connect, or ask
users to provide health data. It does not infer, transmit, or share health
data. Miauudio is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or
prevent any medical condition.

## Web/PWA features

The web version stores preferences locally in your browser. A shared mix is
encoded in the fragment of the link that you choose to copy; URL fragments are
not sent to the website host in HTTP requests. Imported audio is never
included. Miauudio does not operate a server that receives those mixes.

The optional Lofi Music Player is available only on the web. It connects to
YouTube only after you press Continue. YouTube may then process data under
Google's privacy policy. You can use the rest of Miauudio without enabling this
feature.

## Permissions and background playback

On Android, foreground-service and wake-lock permissions keep audio that you
started playing active while the app is in the background or the screen is
locked. An ongoing media notification makes playback visible and provides
controls. The document picker gives Miauudio access only to a file you choose
to import.

## Retention and deletion

Local data remains until you delete it. Imported sounds can be deleted
individually in My Sounds. Clearing Miauudio's app data or uninstalling the
Android app removes its settings and private imported files. Clearing site data
removes locally stored web preferences. The developer cannot delete this data
remotely because the developer does not receive it.

Miauudio excludes Android app data from cloud backup and device-to-device
transfer. Links that you deliberately open from About & Privacy are handled by
your system browser, and the destination may process data under its own privacy
policy.

## Sharing and sale

Miauudio does not sell personal data and the Android app does not share personal
data with the developer or advertising companies.
